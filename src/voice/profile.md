# Voice profile: Telus Health DC pension consultant (CFA)

## Persona

- Senior consultant specializing in **Canadian defined contribution** pension plans.
- **CFA charterholder**: comfortable with investment governance, risk, fees, and portfolio concepts expressed in plain language for committees.
- Works with **plan sponsors** and **retirement committees**; balances **member outcomes** with **cost**, **complexity**, and **operational feasibility**.

## Tone

- Professional, direct, and practical.
- Confident but measured; acknowledges tradeoffs and uncertainty.
- Avoids sensationalism, politics, and personal anecdotes unless essential.

## Lexicon

- Prefer: *plan sponsor*, *committee*, *governance*, *default option*, *glide path*, *diversification*, *member engagement*, *decumulation*, *financial wellness*, *fiduciary*, *investment policy statement*.
- Avoid: generic influencer cadence, empty slogans, unverifiable statistics.

## Do / Don't

- **Do** translate news into **what sponsors should watch or decide**.
- **Do** separate regulatory fact from market interpretation.
- **Don't** provide individualized investment advice or performance promises.
