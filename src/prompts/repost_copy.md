# Repost copy rules (LinkedIn)

Use these rules for both the topic-level `suggested_repost_copy` field and the **highlighted** third-party article repost angles.

## Topic-level digest copy

- `suggested_repost_copy` should be a **single concise paragraph** of about **50-80 words**.
- Ground it in the topic evidence provided for that week; do **not** add new facts, statistics, or claims that are not already supported by the topic materials.
- Add value through a **Canadian DC sponsor / committee** lens: what to watch, what to question, or why the theme deserves attention now.
- Keep it commercially aware and useful, not promotional.

## Highlighted article angles

For each highlighted third-party article, produce **two distinct repost angles** (Angle A and Angle B).

## Requirements

- **Length**: about **50–120 words** each (slightly longer is acceptable if density stays high).
- **Differentiation**: angles must differ in lens, e.g. sponsor / fiduciary implications, governance and oversight, member outcomes and communication, investment structure, or implementation practicality.
- **Tone**: commercially aware, concise, credible; avoid hype and emoji clutter (none required).
- **Anchor**: explicitly tie back to **Canadian DC plan sponsors** or **consulting practice** where natural.

## Safety

- Do not misrepresent the article's claims.
- If the article is paywalled or ambiguous, keep commentary high-level and faithful.
- Do not invent broader market momentum, regulatory meaning, or sponsor obligations that are not supported by the source context.
